A quick update to the blog format

A quick update for all y’all.

I’m hoping to try to post regularly but I have come to realize the difficulty of writing high quality content twice a week. I’m going to attempt to keep this medium going by continuing to post as often I can. The current plan is to post once a week, looking towards Friday - which is hopefully when the Rushmore review will finally come out. (It’ll be good I promise)

Continuing forward, I’m aiming to do regular posts on Friday about serious topics with some other posts mixed in when possible. I’ll definitely keep mixing in as much technology analysis as possible because that is what I am passionate about. I might also put in a plug for any cool side projects I’m working on, just to show off a little ;)

Anyways, apologies for being so slow again. The next post will be on time and I’ll keep trying to adapt to this routine.

I’d like to quote Elon Musk on this one, “No, I don’t ever give up. I’d have to be dead or completely incapacitated.”
