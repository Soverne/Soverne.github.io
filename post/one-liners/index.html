One liners x7

They’re as silly as they are short.

It isn’t easy to be original and even harder to be actually funny. I’ll get straight to it:

  1. I substituted the thyme with time.
  2. I rarely find myself in front of The Rock and A Hard Rock Cafe, but when I do it’s when I’m writing a post about oneliners.
  3. A baby shower at a divorce hearing is no fun for both parties.
  4. A casino goer was punched while at the blackjack table. This was the third run and pun incident this week.
  5. Donald Trump’s humble side.
  6. I always make an effort not to mention Japan while I shop for German Kitchenware.
  7. Manners are as old as the trees, always start your request with “Wood you please”.

In the writing of this post, I realized the value of delivery to a joke. If you didn’t laugh while reading it, try telling it to a friend (and share the blog). Hopefully they don’t get injured laughing out loud.
