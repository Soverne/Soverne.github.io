Hello world!

“A legendary blog begins with a single post” - 21st Century Confucius

Welcome to the blog. I promise it won’t always be this cheesy. I’m Tony, currently finishing my first co-op term and moving into 2A Mechatronics Engineering at the University of Waterloo.

I’m starting a blog so I can write a bit more outside of Facebook messages and class notes. I would also like to share some knowledge about topics that don’t get brought up in normal conversation.

I don’t plan on adding a comment section to my posts but if you have any feedback feel free to send me an email at htonydeng+blog@gmail.com.

The current plan is two write two blog posts a week. One serious post and one that isn’t. The tentative schedule is a funny post on Monday and a serious post on Friday. I’m currently trying to work on my humor so I’m going to be posting a compilation of 7 fun one-liner jokes that I think of during the week. More fun things to come!

Until then, see you all this Friday!
